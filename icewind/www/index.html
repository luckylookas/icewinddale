North of the spine of the world and west of the towering Redhead Glacier lies a rigid expanse that few dare to explore. Most of icewind dales people are outcasts or fugitives or pariahs in search for a place where they are tolerated - if not accepted.
Some come for the solitude, some to make their fortunes. Some come for the people, as the bitter cold nights draws everyone closer. So dress up in your warmest clothes, pack enough food and water and travel up the mountain passes, across the spine of the world and into the unknown.

So traveler, who are you and why have you come?
